About Us

Seekai was founded in 2021 with the goal of making education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or location. Our team of experienced teachers and professionals are dedicated to providing high-quality courses in a variety of subjects, from maths and science to languages and the arts.

Our Teachers

Our teachers are some of the most experienced and qualified professionals in their respective fields. They have a passion for teaching and are committed to helping our students achieve their full potential. We carefully select our teachers based on their expertise, teaching experience, and dedication to their students.

Our Courses

We offer a wide range of courses in various subjects, including maths, physics, English, history, geography, languages, and the arts. Our courses are designed to be engaging and interactive, with a focus on real-world applications and problem-solving. We also offer personalized tutoring and support to help students achieve their goals.
